how donations flow

tied to your animal.
not a vague pool.

pawd. allocates at least 50% of net advertising revenue to shelters through the shelter fund. donations are routed via every.org, a registered 501(c)(3) platform — your org receives a direct ACH transfer and a donation receipt every month.

the donation is tied to the specific animal the user swiped on. it goes to your org — not a general pool, not a charity middleman.

eligibility

who we work with.
