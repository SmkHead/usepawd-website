01
who we are

built on a simple belief.

pawd. is built on the belief that every animal deserves a home and every adopter deserves a perfect match. we're not another pet listing site. we're a personality-first platform that learns what you're looking for and makes the path to adoption feel human.

we're a small, independent team that believes the best products solve real problems with care and intention. pawd. is that product for pet adoption.

02
the problem

adoption is broken.

there are millions of adoptable animals in shelters across the united states right now. most adoption platforms feel clinical, overwhelming, or both — long forms, static photos, no sense of whether this animal is actually right for your life.

the result is decision fatigue. people abandon the search. animals wait longer. shelters struggle to find homes for animals that would be a perfect fit for someone out there.

pawd. exists to close that gap.

03
our approach

personality first, always.

pawd. uses a short lifestyle quiz to understand who you are — your living situation, activity level, experience with animals, what you're hoping for. from there, the app surfaces adoptable pets from real shelters that match your profile.

the more you use pawd., the better it gets at understanding what you're looking for. swipe right on pets you love, pass on ones that aren't right — pawd. learns and adjusts.

every pet profile shows real photos, temperament notes, health status, and shelter contact info. no mystery, no friction. just the information you need to feel confident.

04
the donation model

the shelter fund.

pawd. is free to use. we generate revenue through rewarded advertising — short ads users can choose to watch to earn shelter fund credit. a meaningful share of all ad revenue goes directly to animal shelters through the shelter fund.

the donation mechanic is built into the gesture itself. when you find a pet you love, swipe down on their photo — that's the "swipe down." it sends $0.25 from your shelter fund balance to the specific shelter caring for that animal. no forms, no friction, no rounding up at checkout.

we believe this model works because it aligns incentives. the more people use pawd., the more shelters receive. the more shelters receive, the better they can care for animals waiting for homes.

05
who built this

made in new york.

pawd. is built by a small independent software studio based in new york city. we build products we actually believe in — things that solve real problems with care and intention rather than just capturing attention.

when we looked at the pet adoption space and saw how outdated and impersonal the experience felt, we saw a clear opportunity to apply good product thinking to something that genuinely matters. pawd. is what that looks like.
